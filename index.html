Written analysis

Capacity squeeze in the Midlands corridor is creating a pricing opportunity

Several major operators have reported utilisation rates exceeding 94% across East Midlands and North Midlands facilities, driven by sustained demand from ambient-to-chilled conversion projects and a wave of meal kit and ready meal contracts awarded to regional manufacturers in Q1.

New development pipeline is not keeping pace with demand in the M1 corridor — a structural gap unlikely to close before mid-2026.

Two notable planning applications were submitted in the Northampton/Daventry area this quarter, but lead times from consent to operational typically run 18–24 months for purpose-built cold stores. This leaves an identifiable window for existing operators with spare capacity to price at a premium.
